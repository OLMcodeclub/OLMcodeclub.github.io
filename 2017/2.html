Week 2 - RockBand! 29/09/17

Completing and sharing to OLM scratch studio 2017

Today we will focus on getting the Rockband game finished and showing it to codeclub at the end of the session. Below is one I made! Feel free to make your own tweaks...

One thing that you will need to learn today is how to share your projects to the OLM Code Club Studio 2017.

  • Make sure your project has been saved and "shared"
  • Open the Studio https://scratch.mit.edu/studios/4318422/
  • Click the "Add Project" button
  • Choose your project from the iamges at the bottom
You should also click the "follow" button at the top right of the studio page (see red arrow below) so that you can get quick access to the Studio for the rest of the year.
