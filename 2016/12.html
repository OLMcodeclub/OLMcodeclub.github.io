Week 12 - What is the internet? 27/1/17

And is it different to the World Wide Web?

Many people think that the internet and the world wide web are the same thing. While they are closely linked, they are very different systems.

The internet is a huge network of computers all connected together. (Watch this BBC intro to How does the Internet work?)

The world wide web (‘www’ or ‘web’ for short) is a collection of webpages found on this network of computers. (See this great BBC video on the WWW and Tim Berners Lee) Your web browser uses the internet to access the web.
