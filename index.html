Week 16 - Science Week! 10/3/17

Lets show how code club is all about science.

Science is all about experimenting and trying to work out how things work. In code club we are doing the same (but we tend to focus on experimenting with computers rather than people or animals or vegetables!) This week we will prepare some of our work to shocase at the OLM Science Fair next week.

Todays challenge:

We are planning to show 3 things at the science fair:

  • Random numbers and probability - the dice game
  • Shake race - using the mover kits to create an experiment in coordination
  • Reaction time - using the makey makey to make a game where 2 people compete to see who can hit a button quickest

If you want to stick with Scratch we still have another 6 great projects on the code club site including: Memory, Dodgeball, Brain Game, Catch the Dots, Clone Wars and Create your own world! - remember if you complete all 6 you get a certificate!

Don't forget to add your work to the OLM Code Club Studio: https://scratch.mit.edu/studios/3456235/
